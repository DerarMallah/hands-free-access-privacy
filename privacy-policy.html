Privacy Policy for Hands-Free Access

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

Overview

Hands-Free Access does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This application is designed to help users control their PC without using their hands, offering a hands-free accessibility solution.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No data is stored or transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

The app may request access to system-level controls (like microphone or camera) solely to enable hands-free functionality. These permissions are used locally and are not connected to the internet or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party services that collect data.
